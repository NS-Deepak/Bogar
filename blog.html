High Chlorine Water Risk

The Hidden Danger in Your Tap Water

Chlorine is commonly used to disinfect tap water, but excessive levels can pose serious health risks.

Prolonged consumption of high chlorine water can lead to:

  • Skin problems: dryness, itchiness, and rashes
  • Respiratory issues: asthma, bronchitis, and other breathing difficulties
  • Digestive troubles: nausea, vomiting, and stomach pain
  • Increased cancer risk: chlorine byproducts have been linked to cancer
