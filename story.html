We are from Erode. Erode is the turmeric capital of India but slowly becoming the cancer capital of Tamilnadu.

Cancer Causes

  • Water Pollution

    The textile industry's excessive use of chemicals and dyes has contaminated water sources, leading to increased cancer risk among residents.

  • Air Pollution

    The burning of fossil fuels and emission of hazardous chemicals from industries contribute to poor air quality, further increasing cancer risk.

  • Occupational Exposure

    Workers in the textile and turmeric industries are exposed to carcinogenic substances, putting them at a higher risk of developing cancer.

  • Lifestyle Factors

    Tobacco consumption, poor diet, and lack of physical activity also contribute to the rising cancer cases in the district.
